Children around the world are less aerobically fit than their parents were as kids, a decline that researchers say could be setting them up for serious health problems once they have grown up.
This problem is global depending on the family environment, the school environment, and the social environment of the child. The early years are most important for developing habits. This is because children tend to imitate their parents and the environment. If the little ones don't have good examples to learn from, they could become lazy and start choosing unhealthy food options. As well as that today's children have become more sedentary because of technology, which is an integral part of our modern society. That is why I believe that this extra help and push I can offer could make a big difference to our children's lives. As well as that I have years of experience working with young athletes which is one of the main services I offer at the moment.
