I Am Based In Edinburgh And I Can Help You To Improve Health and Fitness


I felt the thrill of sports for the first time when I was 12 years old and my father took me to the stadium to watch a football match, and this was the moment that has driven my life choices. The next step for me was to join the local football team and my passion for sport kept growing. After high school, I studied business management at college as I thought that this is what you need for success in life. Successfully graduating and working in a variety of business environments for the following five years I could not find satisfaction in it. A few years later, in 2017, knowing that my passion lay in sports, I completed a Master's degree in Conditioning Training. During this course, I met many experts who inspired me. I have worked with football teams in Portugal and Croatia where I attended seminars with some of the best professors in the field. Today I see health through sport as a necessity in everyone’s life and constantly seek to increase my knowledge of sports science in relation to our body and mind.
